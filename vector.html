Vectors (Generators)

Concepts

  • Generator / Basis: The set of vectors you define. They act as the "building blocks".
  • Linear Span: All possible vectors reachable by scaling and adding the generators. Visualized as the geometric shape (line, plane, or volume).
  • Subspace: The geometric span that passes through the origin.
  • Independence: Vectors point in entirely new spatial dimensions.
  • Dependence: At least one vector is redundant (lies in the span of the others).
